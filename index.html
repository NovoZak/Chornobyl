About chernobyl, in short

Chernobyl is a partially abandoned city in the Chernobyl Exclusion Zone, situated in the Vyshhorod Raion of northern Kyiv Oblast, Ukraine. In 1986 on legendary chernobyl nuclear power plant happend a huge disaster which we remember to nowadays: 4-th nuclear reactor exploded, that caused appearing non-living area around city. A lot of historys, legends and games created about this disaster.
